Creatures

A simulation where different creatures interact with the surroundings and each other

Environment

  • Environment is made up of Plants (Moss, Musk) and Creatures (Jeager, Emono)
  • Jeagers feed on Emonos, Emonos feed on Plants, Plants germinate in Creatures' droppings
  • Musk is extremely nutritive but grows only where there is a high density of droppings
  • Upon reaching a certain size, Creatures reproduce
  • All Creatures and Plants evolve
  • Creatures will die if not fed for certain amount of time
  • Creatures will die when they get old

Objective

To create a sustainable pace of evolution given a configurable initial state - you win if your creatures survive over 20,000 years
